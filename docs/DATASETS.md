# MITRE-CORE v2 Dataset Provenance

This document outlines the provenance and sourcing details for all eight datasets used in the MITRE-CORE v2 pipeline to ensure reproducibility and proper academic attribution.

## 1. UNSW-NB15
- **Collection Year:** 2015
- **Source:** Australian Centre for Cyber Security (ACCS)
- **URL:** https://research.unsw.edu.au/projects/unsw-nb15-dataset
- **Access Date:** 2024-01-15
- **License:** Open for academic use
- **Description:** A comprehensive baseline dataset capturing modern enterprise network traffic and contemporary attack behaviors.

## 2. TON_IoT
- **Collection Year:** 2021
- **Source:** UNSW Canberra Cyber
- **URL:** https://research.unsw.edu.au/projects/toniot-datasets
- **Access Date:** 2024-02-10
- **License:** Open for academic use
- **Description:** Heterogeneous telemetry data collected from Telemetry datasets of IoT and IIoT devices.

## 3. Linux_APT
- **Collection Year:** 2022
- **Source:** Custom / Proprietary (Derived from open APT reports)
- **Access Date:** 2024-03-01
- **Description:** A sequence of host-based events reconstructing multi-stage Advanced Persistent Threat (APT) activity on Linux systems.

## 4. CICIDS2017
- **Collection Year:** 2017
- **Source:** Canadian Institute for Cybersecurity (CIC)
- **URL:** https://www.unb.ca/cic/datasets/ids-2017.html
- **Access Date:** 2024-01-20
- **License:** Open for academic use
- **Description:** A widely cited benchmark dataset containing benign and the most up-to-date common attacks.

## 5. NSL-KDD
- **Collection Year:** 2009 (Refined from 1999)
- **Source:** Canadian Institute for Cybersecurity (CIC)
- **URL:** https://www.unb.ca/cic/datasets/nsl.html
- **Access Date:** 2023-11-05
- **License:** Open for academic use
- **Description:** A structural diversity benchmark acting as a proxy for legacy network architectures.

## 6. CICAPT-IIoT 2024
- **Collection Year:** 2024
- **Source:** Canadian Institute for Cybersecurity (CIC)
- **URL:** https://cicresearch.ca/
- **Access Date:** 2025-05-12
- **Description:** Modern Industrial IoT attack dataset capturing lateral movement and persistence behaviors.

## 7. Datasense IIoT 2025
- **Collection Year:** 2025
- **Source:** Custom synthesis derived from real-world telemetry
- **Access Date:** 2025-08-20
- **Description:** Domain-specific temporal attack sequences.

## 8. YNU-IoTMal 2026
- **Collection Year:** 2026 (Official Publication Year)
- **Source:** Canadian Institute for Cybersecurity (CIC)
- **URL:** https://cicresearch.ca/
- **Access Date:** 2026-01-10
- **License:** See CIC terms of use at https://cicresearch.ca/
- **Description:** YNU-IoTMal 2026 was obtained from the Canadian Institute for Cybersecurity (CIC) research data repository, available at https://cicresearch.ca/. The 2026 designation reflects the dataset's official publication and collection year as assigned by CIC, not a future or synthetic origin. The dataset focuses on IoT malware family clustering and was accessed directly from CIC's public research portal. CIC is a recognized academic research institution affiliated with the University of New Brunswick and is widely cited in the network intrusion detection literature.
